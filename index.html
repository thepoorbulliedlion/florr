Introduction
florr.io is a game made by M28, who also made several other games. A list of some are below.
  • diep.io
  • agar.io
  • cursors.io
  • digdig.io

Florr is a 2d flower game where players attempt to reach the top spot on the leaderboard. It's FFA, meaning there are no teams. Players have to fight both mobs and other players.
